# WhaleShark
Sustainable Finance Solutions

Whale Shark Solutions creates sustainable income producing properites on nano-budgets by utilizing minimum risk portfolios in decentralized finance. 

Whale sharks are the largest fish in the oceans. They sustain thier amazing size by means of filter feeding, the process of filtering water in order to capture many small food particles suspended in thier environment. Coneptually using capital gains (food) to fuel carefully managed nono-budget expenses (swimming) to create a financial entity that sustains itself without the expense of capital. This foundation for monitizable properties gives them superior sustainability in uncertain financial times.
